What you need to know before applying...
Please make sure you meet the below requirements before submitting your application:
1. You practise one of the following disciplines
  • Clinical Psychologist
  • Counseling Psychologist
  • Educational Psychologist
  • Industrial Psychologist
  • Registered Counselor
  • Registered Social Workers
2. You are registered with a recognized board eg. Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA), Professional Board for Social Workers (SACSSP), etc.
